Help +
  • Service is enabled by default.
  • ctrl + alt + mouseMove : highlights the content.
  • ctrl + alt + mouseClick : to select the content(also to deselect).
  • delete | backspace : to remove selected elements.
  • Click on "Disable Select", to disable the service
  • Click on "Enable Select", to re-enable the service
  • On page reload, every removed content will be back at its place.
  • Can be used to remove anything that blocks the overall view port.
